# Privacy Policy for QuickVault

**Last Updated:** March 19, 2026

## Introduction  
QuickVault is a secure, offline-first mobile application designed to help users store and manage personal data such as links, documents, images, and text snippets.  

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how QuickVault handles your information.

---

## Data Collection  
QuickVault **does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers**.

All data entered into the app, including:
- Links  
- Text snippets  
- PDFs  
- Images  
- Files  

is stored **locally on your device only**.

---

## Data Usage  
The data you store in QuickVault is used solely for:
- Displaying your saved content within the app  
- Providing quick and searchable access to your data  

QuickVault **does not use your data for analytics, tracking, or advertising purposes**.

---

## Data Sharing  
QuickVault **does not share any user data** with:
- Third-party services  
- Advertisers  
- Analytics providers  

Since the app works offline, your data never leaves your device.

---

## Internet Usage  
QuickVault is designed as an **offline-first application**.  
It does not require an internet connection for core functionality and does not transmit your data online.

---

## Data Security  
QuickVault follows best practices to ensure your data remains secure on your device. However, users are responsible for:
- Securing their device (PIN, password, biometric lock)  
- Preventing unauthorized access  

---

## Permissions  
QuickVault may request device permissions such as:
- Storage/File access → to save and retrieve files  
- Media access → to store images  

These permissions are used strictly for app functionality and **not for data collection or sharing**.

---

## Children’s Privacy  
QuickVault does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy  
This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date.
